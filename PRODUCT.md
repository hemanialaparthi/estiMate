# estiMate - Project Estimation Platform

## Overview

**estiMate** is an AI-powered project estimation platform that transforms your project history into accurate, data-driven timelines. Stop relying on intuition – let real data guide your planning.

---

## What It Does

estiMate solves one of software engineering's biggest challenges: **accurate project estimation**. 

### The Problem
- 60% of software projects miss their deadlines due to poor estimation
- Manual estimation relies on gut feel, not historical data
- Team velocity is rarely measured or tracked
- No benchmarking against similar projects in the industry

### The Solution
estiMate provides:
1. **Multi-source data import** - Connect GitHub repos, upload CSVs, or manually enter project history
2. **Automatic velocity calculation** - Analyzes your data to determine your team's LOC/day and productivity
3. **AI-powered estimates** - Machine learning finds similar projects and predicts timelines with confidence scores
4. **Crowd data benchmarking** - Compare against 1,800+ real projects from other engineering teams
5. **Automatic task generation** - Break projects into 15 tasks across 5 phases with time estimates
6. **Kanban task board** - Visual project management with drag-and-drop workflow
7. **Export & collaboration** - Download tasks as CSV, generate shareable links, collaborate with your team
8. **Enterprise security** - JWT authentication, PBKDF2 hashing, SSL/TLS, and ISO 9001 compliance

## FAQ

**How accurate are the estimates?**
Accuracy depends on your historical data. With 5+ projects of similar scope, you can expect 40-60% accuracy improvement over manual estimates. Our crowd data provides additional benchmarking for better results.

**What if I don't have GitHub repos?**
No problem! You can manually add projects or upload a CSV file. estiMate works with any project data format. We also provide 1,800+ crowd projects to get you started.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Your project data is encrypted and never shared unless you explicitly share projects. We use enterprise-grade security (SSL/TLS, PBKDF2 hashing, JWT auth).

**Can I share estimates with my team?**
Absolutely! Generate shareable links, export tasks as CSV, and collaborate in real-time. Teams can view estimates and manage tasks together.

**What's included in each tier?**
- **Free:** Personal estimates with data import and basic analytics
- **Premium:** Individual power features with crowd data and advanced analytics
- **Team:** Collaboration features for 5+ person teams with real-time updates

**Do you offer API access?**
Currently, API access is available for premium users. Contact our team for enterprise API options and custom integrations.
